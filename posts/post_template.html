Designing Solo RPG Content That Actually Gets Played

Solo roleplaying games live or die on clarity. If the reader cannot immediately see how to begin play, the content will sit unused, regardless of how good the ideas are.

In this post, I want to walk through a few design patterns that have consistently helped players move from reading to actually rolling dice.

These principles apply equally to journaling games, tactical systems, and narrative-first play.
