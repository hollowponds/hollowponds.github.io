A New Trifold Today, and the Video Follows Tomorrow

I’ve released a new trifold today: Starting with TTRPGs and Going Solo.

Lets see what it says about the new release on Drive Thru RPG

Starting with TTRPGs and Going Solo is a short, practical trifold guide for anyone curious about solo roleplaying and how to begin. It explains what solo RPGs are, why they are not the sad or lesser hobby some imagine, and why they can be one of the most creative, freeing, and approachable ways to experience tabletop roleplaying.

Inside, you’ll find clear thoughts on what solo play actually is, why it suits the storytelling and GM side of us so well, how it can help you become a better guide, and how to take your first steps without overthinking the process. Whether you are completely new to TTRPGs or a group player looking to explore solo play, this guide is designed to help you begin.

Created by Peter from Hollowponds Solo Sagas, a solo RPG channel with over 600 videos covering multiple game systems, advice, actual plays, and guidance on the solo hobby. Peter is also the creator of Single Player Mode, the Lancer GME, and the Daggerheart GME, as well as a professional GM. He is widely regarded as one of the leading voices in solo roleplaying today.

This trifold is a simple, useful companion piece for anyone who wants to start telling stories on their own terms.
