A New Trifold Today, and the Video Follows Tomorrow

I’ve released a new trifold today: The Solo Process.

This one ties directly into the new How To video coming out tomorrow and focuses on a simple but important idea: the loop that keeps solo play moving.

Set the scene, ask questions, react, resolve, repeat.

Some solo games give you useful tools but do not really explain the process for using them. That is what this trifold is aimed at. It gives you a practical framework for understanding where your tools fit, how play flows from one scene to the next, and how to keep things moving without overcomplicating it.

If you enjoy the trifolds I’ve been putting out, this should sit very comfortably alongside the rest of the range. It is small, focused, and built to be useful at the table rather than just interesting to read once.
